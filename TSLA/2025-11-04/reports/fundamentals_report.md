### Comprehensive Report: Tesla Inc (TSLA)

#### Company Profile
Tesla, Inc. is a leading American electric vehicle and clean energy company headquartered in Palo Alto, California. It focuses on sustainability through the design and production of electric cars, solar products, and energy storage solutions. Tesla's mission is to accelerate the world's transition to sustainable energy. The company has captured significant market share, becoming the largest seller of electric vehicles globally.

- **Ticker Symbol**: TSLA
- **Market Capitalization**: $1.557 trillion
- **Exchange**: NASDAQ
- **Sector**: Consumer Cyclical
- **Industry**: Auto Manufacturers
- **Official Website**: [Tesla](https://www.tesla.com)

#### Recent Performance Analysis
1. **Revenue Growth**: Tesla reported an 11.6% year-over-year revenue growth in Q3 2025, boosting overall total revenue to $95.63 billion, driven largely by increased vehicle delivery and energy generation solutions.

2. **Cost Management**: The cost of revenue stood at approximately $79.14 billion, indicating an efficient operation with an associated gross margin improvement compared to previous quarters. 

3. **Profitability**: Tesla's operating income remained robust at $7.08 billion. However, the profit margin at 5.31% suggests that while revenue is increasing, the company faces rising variable costs, which could pressure future margins if not controlled.

4. **Earnings Performance**: A recent reported net income of $7.13 billion reflects strong demand for Tesla vehicles, which continues to showcase the brand's popularity and acceptance in a competitive environment.

5. **R&D Expenditures**: Research and development expenses of $1.63 billion reflect Tesla's commitment to innovation, crucial for maintaining competitive advantages in the rapidly evolving electric vehicle and technology sectors.
