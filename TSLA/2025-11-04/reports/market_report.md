### Key Indicators Selected
1. **50 SMA (Simple Moving Average)**: A medium-term indicator that identifies trend direction and acts as dynamic support/resistance.
2. **200 SMA**: A long-term moving average that confirms overall market trend and can indicate golden or death cross setups.
3. **10 EMA (Exponential Moving Average)**: A short-term average responsive to price changes, useful for capturing momentum shifts.
4. **MACD (Moving Average Convergence Divergence)**: This indicator provides insight into momentum changes via EMA differences and is useful for spotting trend reversals.
5. **MACD Signal**: Smooths the MACD line, helping to confirm buy/sell signals based on crossovers.
6. **MACD Histogram**: Illustrates the difference between the MACD line and its signal line, showing momentum strength.
7. **RSI (Relative Strength Index)**: Assesses overbought or oversold conditions to forecast potential price reversals.
8. **ATR (Average True Range)**: Measures market volatility, aiding in risk management and position sizing.
